## Candidate Evidence

### GMKtec NucBox M6 Ultra

- Official current evidence: barebone sale price $269.99 from $399.99.
- Configuration evidence: Ryzen 5 7640HS, up to 128 GB RAM, dual M.2, Wi-Fi 6E,
  Bluetooth 5.2, dual 2.5G Ethernet, USB4.
- Risk: official configured 16 GB / 512 GB and 32 GB / 1 TB variants appeared
  unavailable in extracted page evidence.
- Procurement implication: strong low-cost hardware baseline, but completed
  64 GB / 2 TB / Windows 11 Pro path needs exact cost validation.
- Source: https://www.gmktec.com/products/amd-ryzen-5-7640hs-mini-pc-nucbox-m6-ultra

### GEEKOM A7 Max

- Official current evidence: $699 from $1,049.
- Retail evidence: Best Buy result showed 16 GB / 1 TB Windows 11 Pro around
  $594.15.
- Configuration evidence: Ryzen 9 7940HS and Windows 11 Pro.
- External reporting: recent reporting cited $549.99 deal and support for up to
  64 GB RAM and 2 TB storage.
- Risk: visible common configuration is below preferred 64 GB / 2 TB target.
- Procurement implication: attractive only if preconfigured 64 GB / 2 TB path or
  upgrade pricing is favorable.
- Source: https://www.geekompc.com/geekom-a7-max-mini-pc/

### GEEKOM A9 Max

- Official current evidence: $1,399-$1,629 range from $1,799.
- Configuration evidence: Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 / HX 470 family, Wi-Fi 7, dual
  USB4, dual 2.5G LAN, expandable up to 128 GB RAM.
- Retail evidence: Amazon result showed HX 370, 32 GB / 1 TB, Windows 11 Pro,
  Wi-Fi 7 at $1,399.
- External review evidence: TechRadar reported base HX370 starting around
  $1,399 and 32 GB / 2 TB standard on reviewed configuration.
- Risk: visible RAM is commonly 32 GB, below preferred 64 GB.
- Procurement implication: best polished mini PC lead if 32 GB is acceptable
  temporarily or RAM path is reasonable.
- Source: https://www.geekompc.com/geekom-a9-max-mini-pc/

- Price evidence: direct HP target price not visible in extracted page evidence.

### MINISFORUM AI X1 Pro-470

- Official current evidence: barebone sale price $759 from $949.
- Configuration evidence: AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 470, 12 cores / 24 threads, three
  M.2 2280 PCIe 4.0 SSD slots up to 12 TB total, dual USB4, OCuLink, 2.5G
  Ethernet, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.4, fingerprint, Copilot PC positioning, 86 TOPS
  NPU.
- Official configuration prices:
  - Barebone: $759 from $949.
  - 32 GB RAM + 1 TB SSD: $1,327 from $1,659.
  - 64 GB RAM + 1 TB SSD: $1,639 from $2,049.
  - 96 GB RAM + 2 TB SSD: $2,039 from $2,549.
- Warranty/support evidence on page: free shipping, free RMA return, 0% APR

  adding a 2 TB storage upgrade; the 96 GB / 2 TB configuration is well above

- Procurement implication: technically strong and visually compelling, but
  budget-disqualified for the preferred 64 GB / 2 TB target unless a much larger
  promotion appears.
- Source: https://store.minisforum.com/products/minisforum-ai-x1-pro-470-mini-pc
