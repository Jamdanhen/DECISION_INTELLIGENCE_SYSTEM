## Working Criteria

From the workstation case:

- Preferred completed cost around $1,000
- Hard ceiling $1,500
- Windows 11 Pro
- 64 GB RAM preferred
- 2 TB NVMe preferred
- Intel Core Ultra 5, Intel Core i5 or better, AMD Ryzen 5 or better
- Prefer Core Ultra 7, Ryzen 7, or Ryzen 9 when value justifies
- Wi-Fi 7 preferred
- Wi-Fi 6E acceptable
- Excellent real-world wireless
- Easy maintenance
- Strong ready-to-use productivity
- Upgrade candidates allowed when completed cost, effort, and risk are clear

## Quick Read

The current field appears to split into three practical lanes:

- Value upgrade builds: lower purchase price, require RAM/storage upgrades.
- Compact business desktops: better support and Windows 11 Pro fit, often more
  expensive.
- Mini workstations: stronger expansion and networking, but may exceed the
  preferred completed cost.

No final recommendation is ready yet.

## What Needs Verification Next

For each "look closer" candidate:

- Exact current price from vendor or major retailer
- Whether Windows 11 Pro is included
- Exact RAM configuration and maximum supported RAM
- Exact storage configuration and second M.2 availability
- Wi-Fi version and chipset
- Upgrade parts needed to reach 64 GB / 2 TB
- Completed cost including upgrades
- Warranty and return path
- Real-world reliability and wireless evidence
