- local storage is still useful, but not the sole source of truth
- 2 TB storage becomes convenience, not authority
- lower-cost machines become more acceptable if backup/sync is sound
- seller/warranty still matters, but failure is less catastrophic
- the hardware decision should optimize for reliable local work, not archival
