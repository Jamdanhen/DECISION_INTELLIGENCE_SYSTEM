- seller trust and warranty terms for the target MS-A2 configuration
- return window and shipping date
- whether Windows is Windows 11 Pro, not Home
- whether the target configuration includes 1 TB or 2 TB as one drive or
  multiple drives
- whether storage expansion to 2 TB or more is easy and low-risk
- whether GEEKOM A9 Max has a 64 GB configuration under $1,500
- whether HP direct has a sale that brings 64 GB / 2 TB under the ceiling
- whether MINISFORUM AI X1 Pro-470 has enough incremental value at $1,639 to
  justify exceptional-candidate visibility
- whether MINISFORUM AI X1 Pro-470 96 GB / 2 TB drops below the $2,000
  visibility band

- final tax and shipping for AI X1 Pro-370 96 GB / 2 TB
